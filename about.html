VISUAL COMMUNICATOR.

Jaeyong Lee

UI/UX Design Consultant at Philips

Currently living and working in Shanghai.

Diverse design backgrounds: art, craft, animation, visual design, UX design and service design.
Have been working in international environments: South Korea, Finland, Singapore and China.
Have been awarded 6 times in international design competitions and selected as a full scholarship student of NIIED for M.A.
Constantly learning technologies: at the moment, HTML, CSS and JavaScript.
Higly interested in digital innovation through persuasive design, data visualization and conversational UI.
My ultimate goal of design is creating an impactful digital service, which can positively influence people’s lives and change their behaviors.
About me

New challenges make me alive. From the time when I moved to Finland for my master’s study, I have challenged myself through different tasks and environments. I do not restrict myself by environmental situations or cultural differences. I accept it as an interesting opportunity enlarging my perspective and experience. I am eager to seek new knowledge and people that could make my life more interesting and energetic. Thanks for this attitude of my life, I could develop myself through multicultural experience with talented people.



Visual communicator

With my 4 years of professional working experience and diverse projects during my education, I have worked as a UX/ Visual designer who transforms complex design issues to insightful and effective visual languages. I am very confident at developing digital services from conceptualization to implementation via building information structures, designing complex systems, making UX strategies, conducting diverse user researches and implementing hands-on developments: from sketches to interactive prototypes. I am also a master of most design programs and a quick learner for new technologies, so that I can constantly accept and develop my design capabilities. My visual design aims not only for enhancing aesthetics and usability of products, but also stimulating emotional interactions between users and products that can influence the behaviors of people by delivering optimal experience. Design works have been awarded in various international design competitions. Especially, two awards in Red Dot Design Award prove my design capability and creativity. Those experience has empowered me to be a creative designer whom is confident creating visuals through diverse media: print, mobile, web and Internet-connected devices, to meet the needs of the users, companies and clients.



Experience

One of my recent working experience was in Philips as a design intern. I have successfully worked in actual company projects: creating information visualizations for Primary Care Project and developing UI Design for Philips Online Shop Singapore website. The experience has allowed me to be a designer whom is confident working in new environment with international teams and creating design solutions for large-scale projects. After the experience, I am currently working on my master’s thesis with Philips: Visual Communication for Behavior Change, which will be finalized in the middle of November, 2016. 



Team player

I am a team player who highly values the impact of collaborations with other professionals. I am confident at listening others’ opinions and making the best design decisions throughout facilitating critical arguments in team projects. My capability also allows me to work independently in a tight project schedule, and to handle diverse design tasks at the same time. In addition, as a previous Co-Founder and visual designer in KEIDA studio, I have also learned leadership, project management and communication skills.
