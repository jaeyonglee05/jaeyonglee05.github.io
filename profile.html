Summary

 

I am a UX/Visual designer who endeavors to bring positive changes in people’s lives by transforming complex design issues into persuasive, experiential and effective design solutions.

 

My design capabilities include UX/UI, interaction design, and service design: creating UX strategy, information architecture, UI design, user flow map, interactive prototype as well as qualitative and quantitative user researches and analysis of it.

 

I am confident at conducting digital design projects starting from user researches to the implementable design guidelines through various design methodologies: Human-Centered Design, Co-Design, Lean and Agile UX.
