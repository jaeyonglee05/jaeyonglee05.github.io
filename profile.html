Summary

 

A User Experience designer based on user-centered and experience-driven design approaches throughout design researches and strategies. Has worked and participated in various design projects as mainly a visual communicator for user experience, user interface, data communication, interaction design, and service design. Endeavors to design for optimal user experience and user centricity with design strategies, systematic thinking, and critical perspectives questioning on existing knowledge.

 

Selected as a representative Korean scholarship student by National Institute for International Education(NIIED) for his master’s degree studies in Collaborative and Industrial Design in Aalto University, Finland.

 

Currently working on a master thesis project with the multinational company Philips for ‘Transformative Data communication for Behavior Changes and Mindset’.
