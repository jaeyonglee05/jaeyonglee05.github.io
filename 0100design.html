brand experience design for 0100design

 

Year: 2012

Client: 0100DESIGN

Designer: Jae Yong Lee
Parts of participation: BRAND EXPERIENCE DESIGN
Participation: 100%

0100design is a design studio based in South Korea. The studio works for product design, interior design and graphic design.

 

My task as one of designers in 0100design at the time was building the brand experience: logo design, website design and capturing conceptual images by following the design goal of the studio: emptiness to completeness.

<Logo design for 0100design>

The logo of 0100design represents emptiness to completion, which metaphorically illustrates the meaning in numbers: 0 to 100. 0 is empty, not perfect and everything is possible. 100 is the perfection, fulfillment and completion. As the philosophy of the design studio seek for, the logo design also pursuits the spirit of the philosophy.
